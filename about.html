What are we?

We are a "non-profit organization" that shelters stray dogs.
We usually take in dogs in bad condition (missing a limb/limbs, blind, deaf, etc).
We take good care of our dogs and we make sure to have them in the best conditions so they are very friendly!

If you live in the area and have room in your house/apartment, come adopt one of our dogs! You won't regret it!
